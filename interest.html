She originally became well-known for being the youngest and center member of the project group IZ*ONE, which was created through the reality competition "Produce 48." At 171 cm (5'7") tall, Jang Wonyoung is widely recognized for her captivating appearance, graceful stage manner, and diverse range of vocal and dance skills. Her attraction has continued to attract fans since her December 2021 IVE debut, confirming her position as an influential figure in the K-pop scene.
